Support for Victims of Abuse

Faneuf Law Group LLC – Personal Injury Attorneys in Massachusetts

At Faneuf Law Group LLC, we understand that abuse — whether physical, emotional, or psychological — can leave lasting scars. Our mission is to provide compassionate, strategic legal support to help victims reclaim their lives and pursue justice.

Types of Abuse We Handle

  • Domestic Violence
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Emotional and Psychological Abuse
  • Elder Abuse
  • Child Abuse

Your Legal Options

Victims of abuse have rights that deserve protection. Our attorneys can help you:

  • Secure restraining or protective orders
  • Pursue compensation for physical and emotional damages
  • Hold perpetrators accountable through civil litigation
  • Coordinate with medical and counseling professionals

Why Choose Us

We’ve recovered millions for clients across Massachusetts and are known for our integrity, discretion, and relentless advocacy. From your first consultation to final resolution, we’re by your side every step of the way.

You are not alone. Let us help you take the next step toward healing and justice.
